# Executive Summary: RoadSoS

## Problem
In India, the "Golden Hour"—the first 60 minutes after a road accident—is often lost due to delayed reporting and lack of situational awareness. Over 150,000 lives are lost annually on Indian roads, many of which could be saved with faster emergency response.

## Solution
**RoadSoS** is an integrated IoT and AI ecosystem that bridge the gap between crash occurrence and emergency response.
- **Auto-Detection:** IoT-enabled sensors (Edge Layer) detect high G-force impacts automatically.
- **Edge AI:** Aegis-Core AI filters out false positives (e.g., potholes) using local inferencing.
- **Precision Triage:** Real-time telemetry is streamed to a central gateway, which uses spatial indexing to locate the nearest available trauma centers and dispatch responders with precision severity data.

## Impact
- **80% Faster Reporting:** Accident detection is near-instantaneous, eliminating reliance on human witnesses.
- **Resource Efficiency:** Emergency services are routed to high-severity crashes first, optimizing hospital trauma ward preparation.
- **Scalable Infrastructure:** Built on a monorepo architecture, RoadSoS can be deployed across cities with minimal infrastructure overhead.

## Vision
RoadSoS aims to make road safety a data-driven standard in urban planning, ensuring that no accident goes unnoticed and every life is given a fighting chance within the golden hour.
